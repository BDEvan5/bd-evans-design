Postdoc @ Stellenbosch

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Stellenbosch Univerisity.

After completing my PhD in machine learning for robotics, I have decided to focus on machine learning projects and expand my skill set.

University

In 2016, I moved to Stellenbosch and started studying a bachelor's in mechatronic engineering.

After graduating in 2019, I continued with a masters degree, which was upgraded to a PhD. At the end of 2022, I completed my PhD, called, “Accelerating deep reinforcement learning for autonomous racing”.

During my PhD, I discovered my love for research and machine learning.

School

I went to high-school at Stelborne College in East London, in the Eastern Cape of South Africa. I matriculated in 2015 with academic honours.

During school, my main sport was rowing, I also played rugby, hockey and squash and did debating.

Since I was small, I had always wanted to be an engineer and in high-school maths was my favourite subject.
